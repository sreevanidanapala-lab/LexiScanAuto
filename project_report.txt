PROJECT REPORT
LexiScan Auto – Legal Contract Entity Extraction System
Case Study: Service Agreement Analysis
1️⃣ Introduction

LexiScan Auto is an AI-powered Legal Contract Intelligence System designed to automatically extract structured legal entities from unstructured agreements.

This report demonstrates the system using a Service Agreement contract sample.

2️⃣ Problem Statement

Law firms manually review contracts to identify:

Contract dates

Parties involved

Financial commitments

Termination conditions

Manual review is:

Time-consuming

Error-prone

Difficult to scale

The objective is to automate this process.

3️⃣ Input Contract (Sample Used)
Service Agreement entered on 2024-03-15 between Alpha Technologies Pvt Ltd and Beta Retail Solutions.

Alpha Technologies Pvt Ltd agrees to provide IT consulting services for $300,000.

This Agreement may be terminated by either party with 60 days written notice.

In case of material breach, the non-breaching party may terminate immediately.
4️⃣ System Workflow
Raw Contract Text
        ↓
NER Entity Extraction
        ↓
Rule-Based Validation
        ↓
Structured JSON Output
5️⃣ Technologies Used

Python

spaCy (NER Model)

FastAPI (API Service)

Docker (Deployment)

Streamlit (Dashboard)

6️⃣ Extracted Entities (System Output)

After processing the above contract, the system generates:

{
  "dates": ["2024-03-15"],
  "parties": [
    "Alpha Technologies Pvt Ltd",
    "Beta Retail Solutions"
  ],
  "amounts": ["$300,000"],
  "termination_clauses": [
    "This Agreement may be terminated by either party with 60 days written notice.",
    "In case of material breach, the non-breaching party may terminate immediately."
  ]
}
7️⃣ Entity Analysis
📅 Date Identified

Contract Effective Date: 2024-03-15

🏢 Parties Identified

Alpha Technologies Pvt Ltd

Beta Retail Solutions

💰 Financial Commitment

Contract Value: $300,000

📜 Termination Clauses Identified

Termination with 60 days notice

Immediate termination in case of material breach

8️⃣ Business Value

This structured output enables:

Fast contract indexing

Smart legal search

Risk identification

Automated compliance checks

Time saved per contract: Significant reduction compared to manual review.

9️⃣ Validation Layer

The system ensures:

Dates follow YYYY-MM-DD format

Amount includes currency symbol

Parties follow organization naming pattern

Termination clauses contain legal keywords

This increases reliability in legal environments.

🔟 Deployment

The system is deployed as:

REST API service

Dockerized container

Dashboard-based interface

Accessible via:

POST /extract
1️⃣1️⃣ Conclusion

LexiScan Auto successfully extracts critical legal entities from a Service Agreement.

The system transforms unstructured legal documents into structured, machine-readable data, enabling scalable contract intelligence for law firms.
